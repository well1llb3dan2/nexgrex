## 🔄 Theme Switching

### In Your App
Your settings menu now shows:
```
── Theme Selection ──
✨ Neon Dreams     [Dark/Cyber]
🎨 Vintage Groove  [Warm/Retro]
🌊 Ocean Zen       [Calm/Nature]
🔥 Sunset Blaze    [Energetic/Warm]
🎮 Royal Arcade    [Playful/Fun]

💾 Auto-saves to your profile
```

### Via JavaScript (if you wanted to)
```javascript
// Switch instantly
document.documentElement.setAttribute('data-theme', 'sunset-blaze');

// Persists across sessions
localStorage.setItem('theme', 'sunset-blaze');
```
